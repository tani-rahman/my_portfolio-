## The challenge

Operational teams were making high-stakes decisions across fragmented dashboards. Northstar brings their most important signals into one calm, legible workspace.

## The approach

We reduced the product to three essential questions: what changed, what needs attention, and what should happen next. A modular information system keeps the experience clear as data complexity grows.

## The outcome

The resulting product moves from overview to detail without losing context, with an accessible design system that supports rapid expansion.
